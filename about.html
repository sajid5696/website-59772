About Majed

We are a premier digital agency specializing in modern web design and development, crafting exceptional online experiences that drive business growth.

Our Story

Majed was founded in 2020 by Majed Al-Mansoori, a visionary developer with over a decade of experience in creating innovative digital solutions. Starting from a modest setup in Dubai, UAE, we have grown into a dynamic team dedicated to transforming ideas into pixel-perfect websites. Our journey is fueled by a passion for technology and a commitment to client success, serving businesses worldwide with tailored web solutions.

Our Mission

To empower businesses of all sizes with cutting-edge, responsive web solutions that enhance user engagement, boost conversions, and deliver measurable results in the digital world.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital agency in the Middle East and beyond, where every business thrives through beautifully designed, high-performance websites that stand out in a crowded online landscape.
